Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

At Coordinated ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our SMS-based recommendation service (the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number when you text us to initiate the Service.
  • Message Content: We store the history of your text conversations with us to generate relevant recommendations and maintain context.
  • Location Data: We infer your general location (metro area) from your phone number area code to provide local recommendations.
  • Activity Preferences: We derive your interests and preferences (e.g., "Italian food", "hiking") from your conversation history to personalize our suggestions.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized weekend activity and dining recommendations via SMS.
  • Improve the quality of future recommendations based on your feedback and interaction history.
  • Track clicks on booking links to measure engagement and attribute affiliate referrals.
  • Respond to your support requests or commands (e.g., HELP, STOP).

Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the Service:

  • Twilio: Used to send and receive SMS messages. They process your phone number and message content.
  • Anthropic/Claude: Used as our AI engine to generate recommendations. Message context is processed to create responses.
  • Google Places API & Ticketmaster: Used to retrieve data about venues and events.
  • Supabase: Used to securely host our database.

Data Retention

We retain your conversation history and derived preferences for as long as you use the Service to ensure continuity and personalization. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Opt-Out: You can stop receiving messages from us at any time by texting STOP to our number.
  • Support: Text HELP for assistance.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data by emailing us at ed@coordinatedai.ai.

Cookies & Tracking

Our main service operates via SMS and does not use cookies. The coordination web page (where users vote on plans) uses UUID-based links that expire after 48 hours and does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, sending a notification via SMS.
